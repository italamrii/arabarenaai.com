\# Arab Benchmark AI



Arab Benchmark AI is a web platform for comparing AI models and agents on Arabic prompts.



Users enter one prompt, select 2 to 10 AI models, view all responses, then vote for the preferred answer.



The platform does not show winners or losers. It shows community preferences.



Core features:

\- Arabic-first UI

\- Multi-model comparison

\- Dynamic model selection

\- Community voting

\- Community preference statistics

\- Provider-based architecture

\- Future support for AI agents



Initial models:

\- GPT

\- Claude

\- Gemini

\- DeepSeek

\- Qwen

\- Grok

\- ALLaM placeholder



Tech stack:

\- Next.js

\- TypeScript

\- TailwindCSS

\- FastAPI

\- Python

\- PostgreSQL



Rules:

\- No official ranking claims

\- No winner/loser labels

\- Show percentages only

\- Store prompts, responses, votes, response time, and selected models
